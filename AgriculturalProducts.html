Agricultural Products

Apex Resources Inc. navigates changes in agricultural commodity markets through sourcing products from an extensive international network of farmers, mills, refineries, and distributors. Even in a precarious market, we can consistently deliver among the best prices in the industry due to our relationships and access.

We are licensed by the Office of Foreign Asset Control (OFAC) to trade over 150 agricultural products, including all varieties of:

  • Wheat
  • Sugar
  • Soybean Products
  • Rice
  • Corn
  • Agricultural Oils (soybean, palm, sunflower, rapeseed, cottonseed, etc)
  • Halal Meat Products
